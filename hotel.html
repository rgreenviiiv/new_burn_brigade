Hotels

  • hotels are tricky
  • There is supposed to be a F.S.D. on duty 24 hours, and only one is required
  • But some hotel owners are extremely cheap, and sometimes there are none EVER!
  • IN THE SCENERIO THAT THERE IS A FIRE, you are supposed to wait for an announcement from the F.S.D.
  • A properly staffed hotel will have a brigade, that will report to the floor below the fire, and report back to the F.S.D. VIA warden phone, all the specific details
  • once they are finished reporting they will assist the already other brigade member who is evacuating the fire floor, to safety!
  • they evacuate the occupants of the affected floors 3 floors below of if your on the 6th floor you are to be directed to the 3rd floor
  • A floor diagram(to see where you are at and find the best evacuation route) is in every room
